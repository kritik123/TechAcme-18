About Fest

————

Oriental Group of Institutes, Bhopal is the name that incites awe and respect in the field of technical education. Developed as a family, it has pursued a unique path of moulding bright minds into young technocrats that are Indian in spirit and global in attitude. OGI takes pride in itself as being the “Cradle of intellect”. We are one of the top ranked Institute in Central India. The OGI believes in not only the evolution of the student’s mind but also want them to be creative and active culturally. In an endeavor to bring forth the gleaming radiance of the wonderful world of engineers, the students of the Institute are organizing a national level techno-cultural symposium “TechAcme 2k18” from 25th to 27th October 2018.

It is a conglomeration of events which includes competitions such as Robodino Competition, Assemble-Disassemble, Innovative Design in CAD, Water Rocket Propulsion, Ladder Logic Trivia(Electrical Circuit Model Designing), Circuit Debbuging, Innovative Idea Presentation, Code Hunt, Lan Gaming with PUBG, Lensense (Photography competition). The Cultural program includes Nukkad Natak, Apti & TV Quiz- Sabse Smart Kaun, Mock Parliament, Talent Starlight (Singing, Dancing-Single/Group,Act, Instrumental), Treasure Hunt, Roadies Battle yard, Sketching & Painting, 100Rs/-Venture. Along with these, we are also organizing Aeromodelling Show, Stunt Show and Auto Expo.

The motive behind the event is to provide a platform for hardworking and innovative young minds, to showcase their talents. Creativity and awareness are the things that will be counted here in
“Tech Acme 2k18”.
