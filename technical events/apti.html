About The Event

——

A game based Online Aptitude questions & answers, quantitative aptitude test.

Guidelines

——

RULES:

1. 30 Question will be asked for an aptitude test.
2. 45 minutes will be given for the test.
3. Two marks will be awarded for each correct answer.
4. 0.5 marks will be deducted for wrong answer.
5. 25 students will be selected for GD or buzzer round.
6. 5 students will be selected for Person Interview from the GD or buzzer round.

7. 2 student will be considered for first and second winner respectively.
8. In case of disputes/discrepancies, the organizer decision will be final and binding.
9. The organizer reserve the right to charge any or all the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered participants.
